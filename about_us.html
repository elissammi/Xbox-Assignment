Who we are

We are a video gaming brand created and owned by Microsoft.
The brand consists of five video game consoles, as well as applications (games),
streaming services, an online service by the name of Xbox network, and the
development arm by the name of Xbox Game Studios. Xbox was first introduced
in the United States in November 2001, with the launch of the original Xbox console.

Now, let's get into more details:

Where it all begins

In 2001, the very original Xbox was released. It was Microsot's first foray into the gaming console market. The name Xbox was derived from a contraction of DirectX Box, a reference to Microsoft's graphics API.

The progress

Till today, the Xbox console has come to its fourth generation: Xbox Series X and Series S. Both consoles are capable to run any 3A titles with a nominal target of 1440p resolution at 60 FPS. Besides consoles, we have a variety of accessories such as controller and headset.

The future

We believe that every person should enjoy playing games, even without a console. With cloud computing, everyone could play the most demanding games with mobile phones. At that time, no one will be left behind.

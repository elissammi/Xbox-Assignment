WHAT IS XBOX FANFEST?

Xbox FanFest is currently a combination of exclusive physical and digital events, content, sweepstakes, merch, and more! We are excited to allow more fans the ability to participate with the Xbox community. By registering for Xbox FanFest, you unlock exclusives across Xbox hosted events, promotions, and curated online experiences hosted just for you.


Previously, Xbox FanFest has been a series of physical events taking place around the globe, with limited access. Weâ€™re now expanding this program to deliver fun and excitement to millions of passionate Xbox fans all over the world.

How to register as an Xbox FanFest fan:

  • Register at any time. Must be 18 years of age and older and have an existing Xbox profile.
  • Once you are registered, you can log back in at any time to view upcoming activities and offers that you can opt-in for.
    Check your email for updates on exclusives only for Xbox FanFest fans.
  • Be sure to follow #XboxFanFest, @Xbox on Twitter and other Xbox social channels for updates as well.

What happens after I sign up to participate in an activity?

You will receive an immediate confirmation after you sign up for a specific activity.
From there, you can expect an email that provides details with your access to the activity 72 hours in advance of the activity date.

How often will there be content and activities that I can participate in as a registered Xbox FanFest fan?

FanFest activities and special offers will be offered intermittenly, on an ongoing basis throughout the year.

Does it cost anything to register for Xbox FanFest?

Registering for Xbox FanFest is free.

Are Xbox FanFest registration and activities offered in all countries and languages?

While anyone can participate, we are kicking off with registration, email communications, and activities in English.
